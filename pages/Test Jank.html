Best Adopter Medals award category is back for the 2nd time with expanded criteria of recognising Teams that have adopted past Nationally or Internationally awarded solution(s) outside of their institutions and have demonstrated positive impact through improved efficiency, productivity, and sustainability.

  • There will be up to three Best Adopter Medals with awards of S$1,000 each.

  • Solution(s) adopted may be modified from the originator, to suit the needs of institutions

2nd run of Seed Fund for Winning Teams
A seed fund of S$50,000 will be awarded to each eligible\* winning team for a 12-month period to develop and implement new innovative projects.

  • The seed fund aims to incentivise the winning teams to ride on the momentum of innovation.

\* Eligibility for Seed Fund: Only winning teams (of any of the 4 winning categories above) from MOH/MOHH entities or those who will embark on new innovation projects in collaboration with MOH/MOHH entities.
